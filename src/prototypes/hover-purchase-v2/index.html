Non-Pushy Purchase v2

Five approaches to product discovery that only reveal shoppable items
after the user demonstrates genuine interest.

Core Principle: Buying is a service we offer, not a pitch we push.
Products stay hidden until the user explicitly engages.
A
Pin-to-Reveal
Products are completely hidden. Only after pinning a card do shoppable items appear. Treats saved items as a signal of genuine interest.
Trigger: Pin action
→
B
Dwell Discovery
Linger on an image for 2 seconds and products gently reveal. Quick glances show nothing — no accidental discovery.
Trigger: 2-second hover
→
C
Engagement Ladder
Three explicit steps: Save → "See items" → Product list. Maximum consent, no shortcuts. User opts in at every stage.
Trigger: 3-step progression
→
D
Discovery Toggle
Global "Shop Mode" toggle in the header. Browse freely by default. Flip the switch when ready to see products.
Trigger: Explicit toggle
→
E
Whisper Hint
Like an image, and a quiet text hint appears: "Some items here are available." Click to reveal — like a helpful aside, not a pitch.
Trigger: Like + subtle text
→

Comparison Matrix

Variant Initial State Friction Best For
A: Pin-to-Reveal No indicators Low (1 action) Pinterest-style save boards
B: Dwell Discovery No indicators Very low (just wait) Exploratory browsing
C: Engagement Ladder Save button only High (3 steps) Maximum consent
D: Discovery Toggle No indicators Medium (mode switch) Clear separation of modes
E: Whisper Hint Like button only Low (like + read) Softest sell, natural flow
