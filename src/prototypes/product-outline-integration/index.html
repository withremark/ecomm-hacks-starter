How These Work

Each prototype demonstrates the hover-to-reveal pattern for seamless product discovery:

  • Hover delay (600-800ms): Prevents accidental triggers while scrolling
  • Translucent white fill: Highlights the product area without obscuring it
  • Glowing outline: Creates a clear boundary around the product
  • Product card: Shows details and 1-click purchase option

In production, Nano Banana Pro generates both the integrated scene and an outline mask for precise hover detection.
