How it works:
1. Nano Banana generates a scene with the product naturally placed
2. Nano Banana also generates a mask (white = product area)
3. The frontend loads the mask onto a hidden canvas
4. On mousemove, we sample the mask pixel - if white, show the highlight + card
5. 800ms hover delay prevents accidental triggers
